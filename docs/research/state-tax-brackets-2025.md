## NOTES FOR TAX CALCULATIONS

1. **Standard Deductions**: Applied to AGI to determine taxable income before bracket calculation
2. **Filing Status**: Brackets differ by status; verify which applies to your situation
3. **Inflation Adjustments**: Some brackets adjusted annually (especially MN, MO, OR, WI, VT, ME)
4. **Special Situations**: Some states conform to federal IRC; verify current conformity status
5. **Capital Gains**: Some states impose separate capital gains taxes (CA, MT, WA, etc.)
6. **Phase-Outs**: Some deductions phase out at high income levels (check state-specific rules)
